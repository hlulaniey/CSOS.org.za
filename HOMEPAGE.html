How to lodge a dispute

To lodge a dispute, please download the prescribed form here and complete it.

Completed forms must be emailed to:
GP, LP, NW: gp-complaints@csos.org.za
KZN, FS, MP: kzn-complaints@csos.org.za
WC, EC, NC: wc-complaints@csos.org.za

Registration of Community Scheme

In terms of the CSOS Act, all community schemes must register with the CSOS. Registration must be filed within 30 days of the coming into effect of the CSOS Regulations (07 October 2016) or the community scheme’s date of incorporation according to applicable laws. Registration is filed by completing the prescribed Form CS 1 and can be completed online or emailed to registration@csos.org.za There is no fee applicable to the registration of a community scheme.

To follow up on a dispute lodged/or query the status of a dispute, please call our dedicated call centre on 0800 000 653. For complaints in respect of disputes lodged please email disputecomplaints@csos.org.za.
